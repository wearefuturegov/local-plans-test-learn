Guidance

Check the evidence to provide with your local plan

This guidance will help you decide which additional studies to provide, based on your context and plan content.

Strategic scale technical and design evidence

Include this if you are expecting to allocate any of the following:

  • a new settlement or village
  • a site with 1000 homes or more
  • a large site that’s critical to your plan’s strategic priorities (use planning judgement and check with PINS)

Settlement study with built-up area boundary review

Include this if there are multiple towns or villages in the plan area.

Spatial development strategy conformity note

Include this if you are within Greater London.

Metropolitan open space review

Include this if you are within Greater London.

Green belt review

Include this if there is green belt land in the plan area.

  • a new settlement or village
  • a site with 1000 homes or more
  • a large site that’s critical to your plan’s strategic priorities (use planning judgement and check with PINS)

Green belt exceptional circumstances evidence paper

Include this if:

  • you are likely to alter existing green belt boundaries
  • you intend to create a new green belt
