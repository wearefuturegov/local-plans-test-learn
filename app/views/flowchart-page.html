'Sites' flowchart



1. Are you expecting to allocate a new settlement or village?

Yes: Include a strategic scale technical and design evidence. Go to question 2.

No: Go to question 2.

2. Are you expecting to allocate a site with 1000 homes or more?

Yes: Include a strategic scale technical and design evidence. Go to question 3.

No: Go to question 3.

3. Are you expecting to allocate a large site that’s critical to your plan’s strategic priorities?

Yes: Use planning judgement and check with PINS if you need to Include a strategic scale technical and design evidence. Go to question 4.

No: Go to question 4.

4. Are there multiple towns or villages in the plan area?

Yes: Include a settlement study with a built up area boundary review. Go to question 5.

No: Go to question 5.

5. Is your LPA within Greater London?

Yes: Include a spatial development strategy conformity note and a metropolitan open space review. Go to question 6.

No: Go to question 6.

6. Is there green belt in the plan area?

Yes: Include a green belt review. Go to question 7.

No: Go to question 7.

7. Are you likely to alter existing green belt boundaries?

Yes: Include a green belt exceptional circumstances evidence paper. Go to question 8.

No: Go to question 8.

Do you intend to create a new green belt?

Yes: Include a green belt exceptional circumstances evidence paper. Go to question 9.

No: Go to question 9 on next page.
