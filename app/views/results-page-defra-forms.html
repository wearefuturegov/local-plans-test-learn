Your results

Use your results as a guide, especially if you're still early in your plan-making. You should confirm your final list of evidence with the Planning Inspectorate at Gateway 2.

Core evidence to include

! Warning All planners will need to submit core in-date evidence

1. Plan making and strategy core evidence

  • Growth and spatial strategy, informed by:
    • Spatial options
  • Statement of community involvement
  • Equalities Impact Assessment (EQIA)
  • Development Conformity Note
  • Habitats Regulations Assessment (HRA)
  • Local Plan Viability
  • Environmental Assessment
  • Statements of common ground

2. Housing and residential core evidence

  • Housing and economic study, informed by:
    • Strategic housing market assessment (SHMA)
    • Housing economic land availability assessment (HELAA)
    • Housing trajectory and land supply study
    • Housing delivery study
    • Housing needs of Gypsies, Travellers and Travelling show people
    • Employment land review (ELR)
  • Site selection methodology and site allocations

3. Built design and heritage core evidence

  • Urban design and capacity study
  • Design codes
  • Heritage impact assessment
  • Archaeology assessment
  • Archaeological site assessment

4. Natural environment and open land core evidence

  • Flood risk assessment, informed by:
    • Strategic flood risk assessment (level 1)
  • Water cycle study
  • Green and blue infrastructure strategy
  • Create biodiversity action plan
  • Bio net gain (BNG) study
  • Climate change study, informed by:
    • Net zero evidence
    • Air quality management plan
    • Renewable energy study
    • Carbon emmissions study

5. Culture, leisure and tourism core evidence

  • Retail, leisure and tourism study, informed by:
    • Retail / town centre boundary study
  • Open space and recreation study, informed by:
    • Playing pitch and outdoor sport strategy
    • Indoor sport facility strategy

6. Community services and health core evidence

  • Community facilities review

7. Transport and infrastructure core evidence

  • Infrastructure strategy, informed by:
    • Infrastructure delivery plan
    • Infrastructure capacity study
    • Education need/school planning
  • Transport study

Additional evidence you may need

You may also benefit from including additonal studies. Use your planning judgement to decide if you need these:

8. Plan making and strategy additional evidence

Informing the growth and spatial strategy:

  • Settlement study
  • Built up area boundary review
  • Green Belt Review
  • Metropolitan Open Space Review

9. Mixed-use development and regeneration additional evidence

  • Scale technical and design evidence

10. Built design and heritage additional evidence

Informing the character study:

  • Townscape assessment
  • National landscape management plan
  • Landscape Character Area Assessment
  • Landscape sensitivity assessment
  • Landscape visual impact assessment

11. Natural environment and open land additional evidence

  • Water neutrality study
  • Coastal change management area study
  • Special preservation areas (SPA) and Special areas of conservation (SAC) designations

Informing the strategic flood risk assessment:

  • Flood risk assessment (level 2)
  • Strategic flood risk assessment (level 3)

12. Community services and health additional evidence

  • Noise Assessment
  • Cemetery provision report

13. Transport and infrastructure additional evidence

Informing the infrastructure strategy:

  • Local area energy action plan
  • Future waste data study
